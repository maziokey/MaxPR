What We Do For Our Clients

At MaxPR, we know one size doesn’t fit all, which is why we offer a complete continuum of PR and Marketing services, from brand management and media relations to stakeholder relations and crisis management.

We work with our clients to identify the right marketing mix to meet their objectives. The media-neutral nature of our agency allows us to provide fully integrated marketing communications, utilising every marketing technique and discipline available to us to exceed our clients' expectations.

Whether you need to develop or revitalise a brand, reach out to your customers, develop your distribution channels, raise awareness of your brand or products, increase sales lead generation, launch a showroom, develop your digital presence or engage your employees... we have the expertise to support your objectives.

Branding & Design

  • Brand Management
  • Logo Design
  • Web Design
  • Corporate communications materials

We discover the true health of your current brand and the insights you need to move your organisation forward.

We start off by listening carefully to the views of your senior people, employees, customers and partners. Only when we fully understand where you are today, can we work with you to develop the brand.

We develop your brand concepts, progressed through to a final logo and artwork and a full brand guidelines document, which is then applied across other materials.

We believe in creativity and functionality when delivering websites for our clients. We deliver highly functioning sites with the intentions of the user in mind, fully responsive to any device.

We then project manage the production of the document from start to finish, drafting all copy including case studies and designing the entirety of the document including infographics, brochures, reports, newsletters, flyers, press adverts, exhibition boards, comments cards, display panels, signage and other materials.

Public Relations

At MaxPR, we’re passionate about PR and how it enhances reputations, creating understanding with your target audiences. Our award-winning PR campaigns raise our clients’ visibility within their chosen sectors and beyond.

  • 03 Stakeholder relations

    We develop strategic, targeted communications programmes that support you in delivering the right message to the key stakeholders and influencers that can impact on your project.

  • 04 Crisis communications

    From strategic guidance to hands on support we are there for our clients when they need us most. We provide 24/7 cover for our clients, with both hands-on support and senior strategic counsel.

  • 05 Perception Research

    We deliver both qualitative and quantitative research to establish what our clients' stakeholders think about them, often at very senior levels across Government, business and the media.

  • 06 PR Reporting

    Our elegant, industry leading media relations platform, combines easy to use tools with exceptional analysis. The first of its kind to place an intuitive and mobile interface at the heart of its design.
